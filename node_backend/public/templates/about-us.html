About DisasterGuard

Our project is dedicated to enhancing disaster resilience through the power of data. We believe that by understanding past events, we can build safer communities for the future.

Our Mission

Our project focuses on leveraging historical and static data to provide insights that help communities and responders prepare for and mitigate the impact of natural disasters.

Technology & Approach

Our server utilizes machine learning and NLP to analyze social media data, historical reports, and structured datasets. By identifying patterns, we equip disaster management teams with data-driven tools.

Our Vision

Through research and collaboration, we’re driven by a commitment to enhancing community resilience. By learning from the past, we aim to make a tangible difference in future disaster response efforts.
