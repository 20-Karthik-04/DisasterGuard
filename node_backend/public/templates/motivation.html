Gujarat Earthquake

A devastating earthquake that resulted in thousands of deaths and highlighted vulnerabilities in urban infrastructure.

Indian Ocean Tsunami

A catastrophic event that underscored the need for international cooperation and effective early warning systems.

Hurricane Katrina

Exposed critical flaws in disaster response and urban planning, leading to widespread flooding and displacement.

Cyclone Nargis

A powerful cyclone that revealed the dire consequences of inadequate disaster warning and preparedness in vulnerable nations.

Haiti Earthquake

Caused immense destruction, highlighting the vulnerabilities of densely populated urban areas during seismic events.

Uttarakhand Floods

Showcased the catastrophic potential of flash floods in mountainous regions, stressing the need for real-time monitoring.

Nepal Earthquake

A major earthquake that emphasized the critical importance of resilient infrastructure in seismically active regions.

Kerala Floods

Demonstrated the growing threat of extreme weather events and the necessity of advanced flood prediction systems.

Australian Bushfires

Caused unprecedented destruction, raising global awareness about the escalating impact of climate-driven disasters.

Turkey-Syria Earthquake

A powerful quake that once again highlighted the global need for advanced relief mechanisms and earthquake preparedness.
